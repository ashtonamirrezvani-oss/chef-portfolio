The Story
Behind the

Sophia Lang is a rising talent in the culinary world, recently graduated from the renowned Auguste Escoffier School of Culinary Arts in Boulder, Colorado. Her training emphasized classical technique, sustainability, and bold flavor innovation.

She further refined her skills during a competitive externship as a line cook at Sam's Italian Restaurant in Snowmass Village, where she thrived in a fast-paced, high-end environment crafting authentic Italian dishes under pressure.
