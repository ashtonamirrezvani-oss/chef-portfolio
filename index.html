CHAPTER I

The Chef
Behind the
Apron

Angelina Harbour is a focused and reliable chef who recently graduated from the prestigious Auguste Escoffier School of Culinary Arts in Boulder. She brings strong classical technique, excellent knife skills, and a proven ability to perform under pressure in high-volume kitchens.

Her 6-month externship as a line cook at Sam’s Italian Restaurant in Snowmass Village gave her real-world experience executing 80+ covers per service, maintaining station quality, and contributing to seasonal menu development. She is now based in Austin, Texas and ready to bring consistency, speed, and genuine passion to a new team.
